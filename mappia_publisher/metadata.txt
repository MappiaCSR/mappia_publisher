The laboratory CSR-UFMG use the mappia in many projects (csr.ufmg.br/csr/projeto/), the most recents are: a project with the World Bank about amazon worth evaluation (csr.ufmg.br/amazones), and fire risk areas identification in real time in Brazilian cerrado (csr.ufmg.br/fipcerrado).

    We developed an enhanced map protocol, to serve the maps over any File Storage service. This service does not require any private server. Just create a FREE GitHub account and start sharing your maps online today.
